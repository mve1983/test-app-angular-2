Welcome to Quiz Game!

Rules:

1. Enter a Username (mandatory, but can be different from your login name, just overwrite)
2. Enter a slogan (not mandatory, will be shown in leaderboard)
3. Select 5 categories and difficulties (mandatory, no double category)
4. Answer the questions by clicking the correct answer (multiple choice)
5. Score:
  • Wrong answer - 0 points
  • Correct answer easy - 2 points
  • Correct answer medium - 4 points
  • Correct answer hard - 6 points
